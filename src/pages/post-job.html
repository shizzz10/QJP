Frequently Asked Questions

What is the "Post Job" service?

The "Post Job" service allows businesses to post job openings on our platform to find qualified candidates. Depending on the package selected, your job listing will be visible for a specific duration and may be shared across additional websites for broader reach.

What are the available packages for posting jobs?

We offer various packages tailored to your needs, including weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly visibility options. Each package determines how long your job posting stays active and the number of external platforms it will be shared on.

How does posting a job on your platform help me find the right candidate?

Our platform connects you to a diverse pool of skilled professionals actively seeking job opportunities. By choosing a package, your job post can gain visibility across multiple websites, ensuring it reaches a larger audience and attracts top talent.

What additional platforms will my job post be shared on?

Based on your selected package, your job posting can be shared on various relevant partner websites to increase exposure. These platforms are chosen strategically to target your industry and attract suitable candidates.
