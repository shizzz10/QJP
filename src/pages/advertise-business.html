Frequently Asked Questions

What is the "Advertise Your Business" service?

The "Advertise Your Business" service allows you to promote your brand, products, or services on the Qatar Jobs portal. With this service, your ads gain visibility among job seekers and employers, creating valuable opportunities for your business.

What is included in the Fixed Rate package?

The Fixed Rate package offers unlimited ad impressions and clicks during the specified campaign period. This option is perfect for businesses aiming for consistent exposure without the need to track individual clicks.

How does posting a job on your platform help me find the right candidate?

Our platform connects you to a diverse pool of skilled professionals actively seeking job opportunities. By choosing a package, your job post can gain visibility across multiple websites, ensuring it reaches a larger audience and attracts top talent.

How do I choose the right package for my business?

If you want to control costs and only pay for engagement, the PPC package is ideal. If you prefer guaranteed, unlimited visibility, the Fixed Rate package is the better choice. Our team can help guide you based on your budget and campaign goals.
